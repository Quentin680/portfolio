    Project summary for the portfolio

    Context & objective

    As part of SAE 1.03, completed in a group of four students, we studied how a wireless transmission device works. The objective was to understand the principles of Wi-Fi networks and implement an infrastructure allowing different devices to communicate through several network services. I was more specifically responsible for the network simulation part using Cisco Packet Tracer, in order to reproduce the operation of the Wi-Fi network and validate that it worked correctly.

    Process & methodology

    The project began with an analysis of the assignment and the distribution of tasks among the different group members. My part consisted of creating the simulation of the network architecture using Cisco Packet Tracer. I configured the necessary equipment to reproduce the operation of the Wi-Fi network, DHCP and DNS services, and the web server. Tests were then carried out to verify connectivity between the different devices and access to network resources. Finally, we combined our work to prepare the final project presentation.
