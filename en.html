Reflection · Year 2025–2026

Reflective Review

A critical and honest look at my learning, my progress and my future prospects.

What I learned

This first year allowed me to discover the world of networking in a practical way: from the physical layer (signals, cabling) to application protocols (DNS, DHCP, HTTP), as well as Python programming and website creation. I learned how to configure network equipment, read frames with Wireshark, administer Linux systems and produce professional technical deliverables.

My strengths

  • Rigour in writing technical documents
  • Network infrastructure configuration
  • Team spirit (group SAE projects)
  • Curiosity and independence in research
  • Organisation and respect for deadlines

My difficulties

  • Python programming (complex algorithms)
  • Technical English during presentations
  • Time management on long projects
  • Some theoretical concepts in electricity/signals

My progress

Between the beginning of S1 and the end of S2, I made clear progress in network configuration, Python and technical report writing. The SAE projects allowed me to put theoretical lessons directly into practice, which accelerated my skill development.
